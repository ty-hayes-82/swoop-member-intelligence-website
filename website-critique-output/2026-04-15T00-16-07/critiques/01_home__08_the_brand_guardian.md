   - **Should be:** Cream (#F7F5F2) or sand (#F2ECE1) background to maintain warmth.
   - **Where:** Pricing section near bottom of page.

2. **Pricing Card Design — Slightly Off-System**
   - The three pricing cards ("Start: $0/mo", "Operate + Activate: $499/mo", "$1,499/mo") appear to use a card style with dark backgrounds and white text that, while not bad, feels slightly different from the card patterns used elsewhere on the page. 3. **Some Light Sections May Use Pure White Instead of Cream**

## Brand Consistency vs. Other Pages

Since this is the home/landing page, it serves as the **brand anchor** — all other pages should reference this page's patterns. Key patterns established here that must be maintained across the site:

- **Hero pattern:** Dark charcoal background + Fraunces serif display headline + orange highlight/underline + dual CTA (primary orange fill + secondary outline)
- **Section alternation:** Dark charcoal → cream/sand → white → dark charcoal rhythm
- **Data callout pattern:** JetBrains Mono for financial figures, contained in cards with subtle shadows
- **Voice standard:** Every headline is operationally specific and speaks to GM's daily reality — no page on the site should drop into generic B2B ("Powerful features for modern clubs")
- **CTA standard:** "Book a walkthrough" is the primary conversion CTA — other pages should not substitute "Get Started", "Request Demo", or "Learn More"

**Cross-page risk areas:**
- If inner pages (Features, Pricing standalone, About) revert to generic gray backgrounds (#F5F5F5) instead of cream/sand, it will break brand coherence
- If inner pages drop Fraunces for display headlines and use Plus Jakarta Sans everywhere, the premium editorial feel will be lost
- If inner pages use "Learn More" or "Get Started" CTAs, it contradicts the deliberate specificity established on this home page
