## Revenue Impact Estimate

Here are the top 5 fixes to plug leaks and accelerate pipeline, ranked by impact and effort:

1.  **Standardize the Bottom-of-Funnel CTA (High Impact / Low Effort)**
    *   *Action:* Resolve the friction on the pricing table. If the motion is sales-led, change all three pricing tier buttons to push to "Book a Walkthrough" (or "Book Walkthrough to Start Free"). Do not mix PLG "Get Free Alerts" buttons with Sales "Book a Walkthrough" buttons unless the product can truly be provisioned instantly without speaking to a human.
2.  **Elevate Social Proof with a Logo Farm (High Impact / Low Effort)**
    *   *Action:* Add a prominent band of 4-6 prestigious Private Club logos right under the hero section. Text quotes require System 2 (analytical) processing; familiar logos trigger System 1 (instinctual) trust.
3.  **Clarify the Secondary CTA "Trade" (Medium Impact / Low Effort)**
    *   *Action:* Change "See a sample brief" in the hero to "Download Sample Brief (PDF)" or "View Interactive Brief". Visitors hesitate to click ambiguous links for fear of being trapped in a gated form or an immediate sales sequence.
4.  **Promote Security & Privacy Proof Points (Medium Impact / Low Effort)**
    *   *Action:* Club GMs are terrified of leaking member data (high-net-worth individuals). Take the SOC 2 Type II and Mutual NDA text out of the 9pt footer and turn it into a dedicated "Enterprise-Grade Security" block near the Integrations section.
5.  **Add an Objection-Crushing FAQ (Low Impact / Medium Effort)**
    *   *Action:* Insert a short FAQ directly above the final CTA. Address exactly how it integrates with legacy systems like Jonas without breaking them, what IT resources are *actually* required, and what happens to the data if they cancel. Answer the questions they are afraid to ask on the sales call.
