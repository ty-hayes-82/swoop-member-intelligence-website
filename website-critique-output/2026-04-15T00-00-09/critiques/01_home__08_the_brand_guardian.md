## Off-Brand Elements
While extremely minor, the following inconsistencies should be tightened up for pixel-perfect brand alignment:

*   **Inconsistent Button Arrow Usage (Pattern Consistency)**
    *   *What it is:* Some primary orange buttons use a right arrow (`→`), while others do not. 
    *   *Where it appears:* The Hero ("Book a Walkthrough") and Pre-footer ("Book a Walkthrough") lack arrows. The Mid-page ("Book the 30-Minute Walkthrough →") and Integrations ("Test Swoop on your data →") include them. 
    *   *Fix:* Standardize primary CTA buttons. If we use directional arrows for forward-action walkthroughs, apply them globally.
*   **Inconsistent CTA Text Casing (Voice/Pattern Consistency)**
    *   *What it is:* A mix of Title Case and Sentence case in secondary actions.
    *   *Where it appears:* Hero secondary CTA is sentence case ("See a sample brief"), while the Pricing secondary CTA is Title Case ("Get Free Daily Alerts").
    *   *Fix:* Align to Swoop's standard CTA casing (recommend Title Case for all button containers, reserving sentence case for inline text links).
*   **Low-Contrast Secondary CTA (Color/Component Consistency)**
    *   *What it is:* The outline button in the "Your members feel it" section has a very faint border that blends into the cream/sand background, making it look like floating text rather than a button.
    *   *Where it appears:* Bottom of the timeline story section ("Book a Walkthrough").
    *   *Fix:* Ensure secondary outline buttons on light backgrounds use a distinct charcoal or brass border to define the hit area.
