## The Conversion Audit

**Hero Section**

*   **Is it doing its job?** Yes. "Stop guessing who's drifting. Start protecting your dues" is an incredibly strong, outcome-oriented headline leveraging Loss Aversion. The immediate pricing signal ("Starting at $499/mo.") eliminates massive friction.
*   **The #1 Fix:** Add an explicit audience identifier to the kicker. Change `PLATFORM` to `THE INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM FOR PRIVATE CLUBS`. Immediately below the CTA, add a "Trusted by..." banner with 4-5 recognizable club logos to instantly establish authority.

**"Six Jobs" / Features Section**
*   **The Job:** Translate features into specific, measurable business outcomes.
*   **Is it doing its job?** Masterfully. Instead of saying "We integrate with your Tee Sheet," it says "Fill every slot... Saves ~6 hrs/week." Providing specific dollar amounts ($2,400 recovered, $5.7K monthly upside) speaks directly to the GM's bottom line.
*   **The #1 Fix:** Add an asterisk clarification near the top explaining *where* these numbers come from to build trust, as right now they feel like they could be made up. (I see the tiny grey text at the bottom: "* Metrics represent average impact..." — move a version of this up or make it more prominent so the brain doesn't instantly dismiss the numbers as marketing fluff).

**"Agents" / AI Objection Handling Section**

**Integrations Section**

If you promise a test, give them a test. Mismatched CTA expectations cause high bounce rates on the subsequent form.
