GMs are notoriously overworked, constantly putting out fires. While the middle pricing tier mentions "so your team acts instead of sorting spreadsheets," the page could be elevated by a brief quote or scenario highlighting how Swoop gives the GM their time back, or makes them look like a genius at the monthly Board meeting. The financial math justifies the purchase, but the personal operational relief drives the urgency.
