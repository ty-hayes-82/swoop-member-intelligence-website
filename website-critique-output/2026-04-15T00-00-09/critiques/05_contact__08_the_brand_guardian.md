## Off-Brand Elements
- **Missing JetBrains Mono for Data:** Throughout the page, numbers and financial figures are rendered in the standard Plus Jakarta Sans body font. According to the brand system, figures like `30 minutes`, `7 founding-partner`, `90-day`, `$18K`, `340-member`, and `5-min` should be set in JetBrains Mono to emphasize data-confidence.
- **Placeholder Typo:** Inside the "CLUB" input field, the placeholder text reads "e.g., Pine Valley Golf Clul" instead of "Club". While a typo rather than a strict design system deviation, it undermines the premium, detail-oriented brand standard and must be fixed before shipping.
- **Missing Brass Accent (#B5956A):** While the orange is used well, the dark section relies entirely on the primary orange and white. Integrating the brass accent—perhaps on the pull-quote's vertical border or the form's trust badge icons—would elevate the premium, warm club aesthetic requested in the dark section guidelines.
