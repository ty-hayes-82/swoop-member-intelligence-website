*   **Where is this company located?** The total lack of an office address or phone number will force a cautious buyer to look up the company's incorporation details or search LinkedIn to see if they are a real business with onshore operations, or just a shell site.
*   **How exactly do they connect to legacy POS systems?** Club software (like Jonas) is notoriously closed and difficult to integrate with. A smart GM or IT manager will immediately question the claim of "Zero IT required" and "Seamless read-only API connection," likely searching for external verification of whether those vendors actually allow third-party API access to Swoop.
*   **What is the legal standing of the data handling?** Because the data handling claims are so strong (deletion in 30 days), a diligent buyer will immediately click the "Data handling & security details" accordion and the Privacy Policy to ensure the legal fine print matches the marketing promises.
