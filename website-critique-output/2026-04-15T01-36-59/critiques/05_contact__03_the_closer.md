## The Conversion Audit

**The Page:** Contact / Demo Page
**Its Job:** To convince a skeptical, time-poor Club GM/COO to hand over their contact info and tee sheet data for a sales call.
**Is it doing that job?** Yes, exceptionally well. The copy is ruthless about focusing on the prospect's pain (lost revenue, quiet disengagement) rather than the product's features. The "What happens next" box brilliantly diffuses the anxiety of the unknown, mapping out exactly what the prospect is signing up for. The low-friction form (only 3 fields, no phone number required) shows deep respect for the user's time.
**The #1 Fix:** Align the commitment language. You are asking them to book a meeting, but the form CTA button says "Get My Custom Retention Plan." In conversion psychology, this borders on a dark pattern (bait-and-switch). If a user thinks they are getting an instant PDF and instead gets a calendar link, their trust plummets. Change the button to match the expectation set in the hero: "Request My 30-Minute Walkthrough."
