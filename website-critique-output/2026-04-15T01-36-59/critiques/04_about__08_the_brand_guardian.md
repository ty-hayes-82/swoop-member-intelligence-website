## Off-Brand Elements
- **Inconsistent Display Headline Typography (Critical Impact)**: 
  - *What it is*: The typographic system dictates that Fraunces (serif) is used for display headlines. On this page, the Hero H1 ("The operating intelligence layer..."), the Team H2 ("An extension of your team..."), the Moat H2 ("Why this is hard to copy."), the Proof H2 ("Intelligence in action."), and the Founding Partner H2 ("Join the Founding Partner Program.") incorrectly use Plus Jakarta Sans (sans-serif). 
  - *What it should be*: These must be switched to the Fraunces serif to match the other H2s on the page ("I was the GM...", "From founding-partner GMs.", "Things GMs ask us.", and "See what Swoop would find..."). 
- **Missing Brass Accents in Dark Sections (Minor Impact)**:
  - *What it is*: The Hero section and Moat dark-card feel slightly stripped back, relying almost entirely on stark white text.
  - *What it should be*: The brand guidelines emphasize a brass accent (#B5956A) as a secondary warm tone in dark sections. Consider applying the brass tone to a specific keyword or secondary line in the hero to warm up the charcoal background.
