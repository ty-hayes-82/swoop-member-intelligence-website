*   **Why it fails:** "AI-powered" and "predictive" are buzzword fillers that dilute the concrete value. You’ve already established that the platform uses AI in the hero.

## The Missing Story
**The Staff's Sigh of Relief.** 
The copy brilliantly addresses the General Manager (the buyer) and the Member (the end-user). However, it misses a quick emotional hook for the *Department Heads* (Head Pro, F&B Director). The GM's biggest headache is often managing staff friction. You mention the agents sending an SMS to the F&B director, but you don't capture the human element of it. A quick "Day-in-the-life" sentence about the Head Pro no longer having to manually cross-reference spreadsheets on a Sunday night, or the F&B manager walking into a fully staffed shift because they were warned about a surge, would complete the narrative triad (GM -> Staff -> Member).
