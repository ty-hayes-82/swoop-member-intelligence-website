## The Missing Story
**The "Saved Member" Micro-Narrative.** 
You have fantastic aggregate metrics ("$1.38M dues at risk visibility") and a great clinical example ("Detected James Whitfield resignation risk 6 days before..."). But the site could use a brief, one-paragraph "Day in the Life" story showing *how* a GM actually uses the morning brief to save a member. 

*Example concept:* "At 7:00 AM, you see John Doe hasn't played in 4 weeks and his F&B spend dropped 80%. At 7:15 AM, you text him to offer a complimentary drink at the turn this Saturday. At 7:30 AM, you’ve retained $15,000 in annual dues. All before your first cup of coffee is empty." This visualizes the *action* taken from the data.
