## Off-Brand Elements
- **Missing JetBrains Mono on Data Figures** 
  - *What it is:* The large orange statistics in the "Intelligence in action" section ("6 days", "91%", "$312", "$1.38M") and the Moat card ("46", "12") are set in the primary proportional sans-serif.
  - *What it should be:* Per brand standards, JetBrains Mono must be used for numerical data and financial figures to emphasize Swoop's identity as a data-confident intelligence platform. 
  - *Where it appears:* Mid-page "Intelligence in action" cards and the dark "Why this is hard to copy" card.

- **Inconsistent Button Casing**
  - *What it is:* The main navigation and hero CTA button reads "Book a Walkthrough" (Title Case), while the bottom pre-footer section uses "Book a Walkthrough" and the mid-page CTA uses "Claim a Founding Partner Spot →" (Title Case) but the section right below quotes uses "Book a 30-Minute Walkthrough →" (Title Case with hyphen). This is a very minor nitpick, but the pattern should be perfectly identical across identical actions.
  - *What it should be:* Standardized casing and arrow usage for primary CTAs across the entire page.
  - *Where it appears:* Comparing Hero/Nav CTAs vs. the inline "Ready to see this for your club?" CTA.

- **Background Image / Stock Element in Pre-Footer**
  - *What it is:* There is a subtle, dark background image of a golfer swinging behind the "See what Swoop would find at your club" section. 
  - *What it should be:* While well-blended and not highly offensive, Swoop's brand standard dictates avoiding generic stock photography. If this isn't a custom Swoop asset, it risks feeling slightly off-brand compared to the strict, data-driven aesthetic of the rest of the page. Pure charcoal with brass noise or geometric accents is usually preferred over stock silhouettes.
  - *Where it appears:* Final dark block above the footer.
