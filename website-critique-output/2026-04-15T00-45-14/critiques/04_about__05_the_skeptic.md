## The Due Diligence Gaps

A skeptical GM would immediately do the following after visiting this page:

1. **Google "Swoop Club Intelligence"** — and likely find little or nothing, given the Vercel staging URL suggests no production domain exists yet.

2. **Search LinkedIn for Kyle Fogle, Jordan Mitchell, Alex Chen** — to verify these are real people with real club/hospitality backgrounds. If profiles don't exist or don't match, trust collapses.

3. **Search LinkedIn for the testimonial authors** — to verify they're real GMs at real clubs. If the quoted names don't appear as club GMs, the testimonials become suspect.

4. **Ask peers in CMAA or NCA networks** — "Has anyone heard of Swoop?" If the answer is no from everyone, the GM will not proceed.

5. **Look for Jonas/ClubEssentials integration documentation** — The FAQ implies compatibility, but there's no technical documentation, API docs, or integration partner page to verify this.

6. **Ask for a live product demo or sandbox** — The complete absence of product visuals means the buyer MUST get on a call to see anything, which is high-commitment for an unproven vendor.

7. **Ask for references they can call directly** — The testimonials are a start, but a serious buyer wants to make a phone call to a current user without the vendor on the line.

8. **Ask about data security, compliance, and liability** — What happens to member data? Who owns it? What are the breach notification procedures? Is there cyber insurance?

9. **Ask about the company's financial viability** — Founded 2025, no visible investors, no revenue signals. Will this company exist in 12 months?

10. **Ask what happens if they shut down** — Data portability, export, and contract termination terms are nowhere to be found.
