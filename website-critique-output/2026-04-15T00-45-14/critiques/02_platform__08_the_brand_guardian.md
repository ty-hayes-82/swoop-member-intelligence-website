**2. Neutral Background Drift — White vs. Cream (HIGH IMPACT)**
- Several content sections ("Six jobs", "One page replaces four logins", "Why one page") appear to sit on pure white (#FFFFFF) backgrounds rather than the brand-specified cream (#F7F5F2) or sand (#F2ECE1). The Swoop neutral palette is intentionally warm — pure white reads colder and more generic. At least 3-4 sections appear to be on #FFFFFF when they should alternate between cream and sand.

The "Six AI agents" section and "Your tools store the data" section could benefit from brass as a secondary accent (for eyebrow labels, subtle borders, or secondary text highlights in dark contexts). Currently, orange carries all accent duties, which flattens the palette.

**4. Eyebrow Label Inconsistency (MEDIUM IMPACT)**
