## The Due Diligence Gaps

A skeptical club GM would immediately research the following after visiting this page:

1. **"Swoop Club Intelligence" + LinkedIn** — Who founded this? Who works there? Does the company even have a LinkedIn page? Are there real employees?

2. **"Swoop club intelligence reviews"** — Any independent reviews, forum mentions, or CMAA (Club Management Association of America) community discussion?

3. **Named client verification** — The GM would want to call a peer. The site gives them zero names to call. They'd ask their network: "Anyone heard of Swoop?"

4. **Product maturity** — Is this shipping software or a landing page for a product in development? The Vercel URL and lack of real screenshots raise this question.

5. **Data security posture** — Where is member data stored? Who has access? Is there a SOC 2 report? A DPA available?

6. **Contract terms** — The $0/mo tier — what are the limitations? What happens to my data if I cancel? Is there a minimum commitment on the $499 or $1,499 tiers?

7. **Integration specifics** — "Tee Time system intelligence" — which tee sheet systems? Club Prophet? Northstar? ForeTees? Jonas? The page doesn't name a single specific integration partner.

8. **The "$42.2K" claim** — What does this represent? Revenue recovered? Revenue influenced? Over what period? For what size club? Completely unverifiable as presented.

9. **Company registration and legal entity** — What is the actual company name? Where is it incorporated? Is there a terms of service?

10. **CMAA / NCA / industry presence** — Has Swoop presented at any club industry conferences? Any trade publication coverage? Any association endorsements?
