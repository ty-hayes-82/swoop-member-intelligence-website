## Brand Consistency vs. Other Pages

Since this is the home/landing page evaluation, it serves as the **brand baseline** against which all other pages should be measured. Key patterns established here that must be consistent across the site:

- **Hero pattern**: Dark charcoal background, Fraunces serif headline with orange italic accent line, dual CTA (primary orange + secondary outline)
- **Section band rhythm**: Dark → Cream → Sand → Dark → White → Dark alternation
- **Data callout pattern**: JetBrains Mono for financial figures, large scale, paired with contextual label

- **CTA hierarchy**: Orange fill button (primary) with dark text, outline/ghost button (secondary) — this must be identical on every page

**Key risk for cross-page drift**: The inconsistent serif/sans headline usage on this page itself means sub-pages may inherit either pattern unpredictably. This should be resolved at the design system level before auditing downstream pages — establish a clear rule: all section display headlines (≥26px) use Fraunces; all UI/body/card headlines use Plus Jakarta Sans.
