## Brand Consistency vs. Other Pages
The primary deviation on this page compared to a standard Swoop layout is the inconsistent application of the typographic hierarchy. Typically, the Serif/Sans/Mono split is strictly delineated by function (Header/Body/Data). On this Pricing page, Plus Jakarta Sans has "swallowed" the role of the other two fonts in the mid-page and lower-page sections. Correcting the h2/h3 tags to Fraunces and targeting the pricing numerals with JetBrains Mono will instantly bring this page to a 95+ compliance score.
