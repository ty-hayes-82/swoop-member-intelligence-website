## The Missing Story

Right now, this pricing page is flawlessly optimized around *loss aversion*—stopping churn, protecting dues, recovering at-risk revenue. However, the system also surfaces "demand signals" and aims to improve F&B (Food & Beverage) revenue. The page currently lacks a brief narrative beat showing how Swoop doesn't just stop members from leaving, but actually gets current members to spend more. Adding a small metric or a secondary quote to the calculator section about increasing average member spend (e.g., "Swoop noticed the Joneses stopped dining on Fridays; an automated free appetizer offer brought them back and increased their monthly F&B spend by 20%") would shift the story from pure defense to exciting offense.
