This directly violates the brand standard: *"Is white-on-orange avoided in favor of dark-on-orange for WCAG compliance?"* It should be dark charcoal text on the orange background, or simply orange text on the section's white background.
*   **Inconsistent Eyebrow Component Pattern (Medium Visibility):** Across the page, eyebrows (e.g., "WHY WE BUILT THIS", "PROOF", "MOAT") are consistently styled as floating, small uppercase tracking text with no background. However, the "FOUNDING PARTNER PROGRAM" eyebrow introduces a solid filled pill container. This breaks the established component pattern.
*   **Potential Neutral Tone Drift (Low Visibility):** The background of the team member cards ("Who You'll Work With") appears slightly cool/gray against the pure white page background. Ensure these cards are utilizing the brand's sand (#F2ECE1) or cream (#F7F5F2) hex codes rather than defaulting to a generic Tailwind gray (like #F3F4F6).
