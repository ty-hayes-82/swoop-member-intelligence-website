## What Would Make Me Leave

I've been through two implementations that promised "seamless integration" and delivered 6 months of IT headaches. Vague integration claims are a red flag, not a feature.

2. **The density of CTAs and competing conversion paths.** When I see "Get Free Daily Alerts" alongside "Book a Walkthrough" alongside "Print this ROI report" alongside "Calculate your ROI" alongside "See the Full Platform" — I start to feel like I'm being worked. My guard goes up. I want to feel like you're guiding me, not trapping me. A single clear path with one obvious next step would keep me engaged; this many options makes me hesitate.

3. **No visual proof of the actual product.** I see a nicely designed ROI calculator widget and pricing cards, but I don't see a single screenshot of the actual Swoop dashboard, the signals it surfaces, or the member app. For $499-$1,499/month, I want to see what I'm buying before I give you 30 minutes of my time.
